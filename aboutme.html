Hey there!

I'm a digital product designer based in Atlanta, GA. I currently work with the incredible folks at The Home Depot's Enterprise UX team, designing software solutions that run Supply Chain operations at Home Depot's fulfillment centers.

I chose to become a designer because solving problems for other people makes me happy and satisfied. Being a designer puts me in a position to use my creative and leadership skills to solve complex people and business problems and add value to the world. To make lives better. And contribute my tiny bit towards pushing the human race forward.

I care, because I see a world where it is possible for everyone to be successful and happy and I want to help make it happen.

If you're interested in collaborating or just want to have a conversation about something interesting, I'd love to connect!
