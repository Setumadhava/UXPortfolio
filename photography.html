Photography

Photography has been the guiding light that has led me to the world of UX. My desire to produce images that tell story helped me explore different cities, countries, understand its people, their culture & history. Photography also taught me important skills like attention to detail, compostion, importance of color (or the lack of it), telling stories through images, and a lot of patience. It also opened opportunities to learn tools like Photoshop which eventually got me to explore the wonderful world of experince design. This page is a collection of some of my selected pictures.
