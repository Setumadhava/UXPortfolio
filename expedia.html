Introduction

For my internship project Expedia provided me with a chance to work on some of the problems they had dicovered through their research. I found the 'Beds & Occupancy' problem most interesting.

How can Expedia's customers customize bed and occupancy configurations while shopping for hotel rooms?

DESIGN PROCESS

Process

Research

Ideation

Design

Period

June 2016 - September 2016

Team

Individual Project. Collaborated with Expedia's Hotel UX Design & Content Team

Problem & Goals

The problem

Research suggested that, when shopping for multiple hotel rooms for a large group (>4 members), users said they could not pick multiple hotel rooms of different types and customize them individually based on their specific needs.

Project Goals

Help users shop for hotel rooms of multiple types and rates in the same booking session.

Provide a clear understanding of available bed types within the room(s) for users when shopping for hotel rooms.

Provide an ability to filter and choose rooms with different bed types.

Improve the overall hotel shopping experience.

Proposed Solution

After exploring multiple variants and possible flows, I came up with a final design recommendation that made use of multiple patterns I had explored.

 

Research

Conducted Literature survey of previous study reports and tear-downs of other online travel agencies to understand the hotel shopping processes used by them.
