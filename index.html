About Me

Hi, I’m Setu. I have three years of experience working as a designer on a broad range of UX projects. In 2014 I quit my job as a telecom engineer to follow my passion in product design. Since then, I co-founded Eventosaur where I designed a platform for planning traditional events in India, interned as an Interaction Designer at Expedia Inc. exploring design improvements for their hotel shopping UX, and as a student of Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington, worked on several individual and team projects involving Usability Studies, Data Science, Interaction Design and Physical Prototyping.

My strength lies in the intersection of my ability to learn quickly and design tangible interfaces & solutions. I greatly enjoy designing interactions & interfaces that can make a positive difference in users' lives.

My other productive activities involve learning classical music, experimenting with new vegetarian recipes, reading books on design & science fiction or watching science videos on YouTube.
