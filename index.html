Hi There!

I’m Setu, a digital product designer based out of Seattle. I love designing products and services that address problems in people’s lives.

The best part about being a designer is the opportunty I get to constantly learn something new about a problem, technologies or skills and use them to make people's lives easier.

My Design Process

For me, design is about solving problems that are about improving the experience of doing a task. I let the problem & the context inform my design decisions and make sure everything I design serve a specific purpose without distracting the users from their goals.

I pick the research methods & design guidelines depending on who I’m designing for or what problem I’m solving.

1. Research

Use research methods to empathize with the users & understand the problems they’re facing

2. Ideation

Explore concepts & hypotheses that can addressed the identified problems

3. Wireframes

Design wireframes and conduct usability studies with users to validate hypotheses

4. Iterate

Iterate on the prototypes based on the usability study learnings

5. Visual Design

Design style-guides & production-ready mockups of the validated solution

Communicate

Work closely with developers & PMs to ensure design feasibility & project timelines
