About

I’m a digital product designer based in Atlanta. I enjoy working with diverse teams on complex problems and design products that make a positive difference in people’s lives. I believe good design happens with the right collaboration, asking the right questions, building strong relationships and solving the right problem. Through various research and design methods, I help teams get a better clarity and understanding of their users, the problems they're addressing and ultimately reduce the risks involved in building products.

Learn more about my design process.

When I’m not working, I spend my time experimenting with cooking, reading books, building aircraft models and listening to podcasts.
